Lago Lagazzuolo hikeSummer~40 min drive
Steep out-and-back from San Giuseppe in Valmalenco to a turquoise alpine lake. About 5 km, 620 m gain, 3–3.5 hours. Worth it for the colour of the water. About 40 min by car.

DifficultyHard · steep

Ghiacciaio Fellaria HikeSummer~1 h drive
Glacier lagoon with icebergs, ice caves and waterfalls above Campo Moro in Valmalenco. Park at Campo Moro; glacier views from Rifugio Bignami, or keep going to the lagoon. About 1 h by car.
Details
DifficultyModerate · shorten as you like
Gear / rentalsHiking shoes, poles. The road up to Campo Moro is narrow and winding; start early. A GPS track helps if you continue to the lagoon.
Beginner pathPark at Campo Moro and walk up to Rifugio Bignami (under an hour): behind the hut you already get a big glacier view. Keep going on Percorso Glaciologico C to the lagoon if you want more. Stay off the ice.

Rock climbing at SassellaSummerNear · drive
Local sport crag right behind Iperal: 50–100 bolted routes on schist, from 3a to 7b (slabs, vertical and steep walls, mostly crimps). Drive over: plenty of free parking nearby, two-minute approach; maps nearby with routes well marked.
Details
Difficulty3a–7b · sport
Gear / rentalsBring climbing gear. Free parking near the crag.
Beginner pathRoutes up to 5c and the 6a–6c band are the sweet spot here. Start on the easier slabs; the on-site maps make it easy to find your line.

Valtellina wine tastingSummerNear · walk or drive
Italy's largest terraced vineyard area — DOC and DOCG Nebbiolo along the Wine Road and Via dei Terrazzamenti, with cellar-door tastings near Sondrio. The trail is also lovely for a run or walk.
Valtellina wine & food
Sentiero ValtellinaYear-roundWalk · in town
Cycle-and-walk path along the Adda, 114 km from Colico to Bormio. The Sondrio stretch (Forcola to Castello dell'Acqua) is almost all flat, past Castellina Park and the Bordighi nature oasis. Great for a run or a bike ride.
Details
DifficultyEasy · for everyone
Gear / rentalsRunning shoes or a bike. Rent a Bike points along the path (including the white Sozzani bridge in Albosaggia).
Beginner pathThe local stretch is almost flat (one short climb at Castello dell'Acqua). Great for families: Selvetta to San Giacomo di Teglio. Open year-round on the local stretch (watch for ice in winter). The Via dei Terrazzamenti wine trail above town is another great run.
Sentiero Valtellina Open map
Bormio thermal bathsYear-round~1 h drive
Three spas fed by nine hot springs — Roman-era Bagni Vecchi, Art Nouveau Bagni Nuovi, and Bormio Terme in town. About an hour away. A favourite after a day on the slopes.
